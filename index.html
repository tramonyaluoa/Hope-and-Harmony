    About Us

    We are Hope and Harmony Foundation Centre, with Registration No : 255-280.
    A community foundation centre based in Daveyton, Benoni. We have been providing support services to our community since 2021. We realized the need in our community after the devastation brought to us by the Covid-19 pandemic.


    OUR VISION


    To provide the best possible assistance in developing children and youth into outstanding and responsible citizens, cultivate the spirit of Ubuntu in them and equip them with tools to make sound future decisions. Provide learners with support on the journey to realizing their dreams and potential as well as providing learners with a safe space for expression.


    OUR OBJECTIVES


    To help learners attain basic services needed for everyday functioning.


    To educate and equip learners with social skills required to succeed in the fourth industrial revolution.


    To advocate for children’s rights.


    To inculcate into learners minds’ the spirit of self-belief and ideals of kindness, confidence and courage.

    Our Services

    We have a Drop-In / Walk-In Centre which provides all our community members with exceptional and distinguished support services through the assistance of Volunteers, Schools and Partners who work tirelessly to provide Hope and Harmony to our beloved community.

    WE PROVIDE SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE FOLLLOWING:-

    CHILDREN BENEFECIARIES

    • Provision of daily nutritious meals
    • After-care with homework assistance
    • Assist with social grant support
    • Opportunity for play
    • Educational stimulation through various educational materials.
    • Educational tours/ outings for children
    • Invite child friendly speakers e.g. police ,child abuse agencies, firefighters
    • Administer treatment for HIV positive children
    • Social Service referrals i.e. Home affairs and SASSA

    ADULT BENEFICIARIES

    • Support groups
    • Income generation/ poverty relief programs
    • Counseling services
    • Information sessions
    • Social Integration
    • Holiday Programme
    • Guide and assist Children with personal hygiene and grooming
    • Create opportunities for learning of social etiquette

    PSYCHOSOCIAL SUPPORT

    • Lay Counseling
    • Support Groups Forums
    • Education counseling
    • Poverty relief programme
    • Social Integration
    • Information session
